Solar Oven For Impoverished Families

We will utilize sunlight to trap photons for our solar powered oven. We will use cork, a sustainable and renewable material that has an thermal resistance of approximately R-4 per inch, as the base of our prototype. In addition, we will use aluminum foil for our lining which has a thermal emissivity (the ratio of the energy radiated from a material's surface to that radiated from a perfect emitter) of around 0.03. Since emissivity and reflectivity are inversely proportional, aluminum will be a very good reflector of heat.
