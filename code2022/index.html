Our Mission

The Davis Engineering Team was founded on the basis of helping our local community and solving global problems. We strive to create goal-oriented projects using science, mathematics, and engineering that will further our mission in problem solving. We also host a variety of events such as workshops, competitions, and hands-on activities.

Autonomous Vehicle Using Arduino & Ultrasonic Sensors

Despite the name, “self-driving cars”, most vehicles are still not fully autonomous. For this project, we decided to build an autonomous vehicle using an Arduino UNO microcontroller and multiple ultrasonic distance sensors to further pursue the endless applications to fully autonomous vehicles.
